Our Story — Born from a Passion for Style

VELOUR was founded in 2018 with a simple but powerful belief: every person deserves to feel confident, elegant, and authentically themselves through the clothes they wear. What started as a small boutique in Brooklyn has grown into a global fashion destination trusted by over 10,000 customers in 50+ countries.

Our founders grew frustrated with the gap between luxury aesthetics and accessible pricing. They set out to bridge that gap — curating collections that blend premium materials, thoughtful design, and fair pricing without ever compromising on quality.

Today, VELOUR partners with over 500 independent designers and ethical manufacturers worldwide to bring you clothing that doesn't just look good — it feels good to wear and is made responsibly.

Our Mission & Vision

The principles guiding everything we do

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize high-quality fashion by making premium, ethically sourced clothing accessible to everyone — regardless of their budget. We believe style is a right, not a privilege, and we work every day to ensure our collections reflect the diversity and vibrancy of our global community.

🌟

Our Vision

To become the world's most loved and trusted fashion destination — a place where creativity, sustainability, and inclusivity converge to shape the future of style. We envision a world where fashion empowers individuals to express their identity boldly and responsibly.
